THE STORY

Brusta’s journey began when Boat Chonatee grew frustrated about the range of breast pumps that she and other Thai mothers had to choose from. An advocate of the goodness of mother’s milk she didn’t agree with brands who were charging a high premium while the cheaper models were simply disappointing in comfort and reliability.

So she started looking into breast pumps and soon found partners in manufacturing and design which resulted in our first Smart Electric Pump; MiracleTwo. A few weeks into testing the pump, Boat was overcome by joy, having experienced her own breast milk miracle with a model that was nearly half the price of international leaders and offered more benefits such as whisper quiet pumping, Residual Milk Expression phase, lightweight but durable built and great comfort.

Today Boat can’t be separated from her MiracleTwo pump and continues to develop magical products with the goal to give every Thai mother joy and pride by achieving her mother’s milk miracle.

BELIEVE IN MIRACLES
Helping mums experience mother milk miracles

Believe in miracles is our message to young and first-time mothers who always worry about not being able to express enough mother’s milk for their babies. At Brusta we make it our priority to make you feel comfortable about the breastfeeding aspect of your journey from woman to mother. It is a scary and challenging process but ultimately one the most beautiful and rewarding experience in life.

RESIDUAL MILK EXPRESSION
Caring for the No. 1 problem of pumping mums

Through research and face to face interaction with mums we discovered that
blocked ducts and Mastitis pose the number one problem for pumping mums.
So we dedicated ourselves to developing a special suction pattern to help mums
cleanse and express the “stray” residual milk in their ducts. Plenty of trials and
adjustments later we found the perfect rhythm and called it R.M.E. or
Residual Milk Expression which builds the foundation of
Brusta’s proprietary 3 Phase expression today.

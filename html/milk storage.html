BREAST MILK STORAGE
The best place to keep your treasure safe

Brusta’s breast milk storage bags are made under strict supervision to meet international safety regulations. Our materials meet the JFSL 370 safety standard for food grade plastics under the Japanese Food Sanitation Law which is the equivalent to Europe’s EC 1935/2004 Standard.

Additionally we have also developed our MagicFresh Nano coating that uses German TOPAS olefin copolymers to enhance the milk’s internal sealing from outside factors which would reduce the milk’s storage time.

MagicFresh MILK POUCH
Keeps everything safe and mess-free
Our MagicFresh Milk Pouch uses a patented design that reduces contact areas between milk and hands in order to prevent unnecessary bacteria interaction. The design also cleverly improves the transfer of thawed milk into bottles via a rigid spout and adds a straw guide to ease drinking right out of the bag.
